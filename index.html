About Us

Crown Cafe & Restaurant is a vibrant, trendy hub located in the heart of Shahjahanpur near the iconic Ghanta Ghar & Bisrat road. Known for its modern aesthetic—complete with stylish wood paneling and glowing neon signs—it’s the go-to spot for foodies looking for a mix of North Indian classics and "Instagrammable" fast food.
Whether you're stopping by for their signature Special Crown Maggi and Tandoori Tea, or hosting a birthday bash in their dedicated party hall, the cafe balances a cozy atmosphere with high-energy service. It’s a versatile space that’s just as perfect for a quiet afternoon of work with free Wi-Fi as it is for a lively evening out with friends.
At a Glance:
Vibe: Modern, casual, and party-ready.
Must-Try: Sandwiches, specialty Shakes, and Fusion Maggi.
Best For: Birthdays, kitty parties, and quick hangouts.
Service: Dine-in, takeaway, and reliable delivery via Zomato/Swiggy.
